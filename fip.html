FIP

Established in 1971 FIP originally France Inter Paris radio plays an ecletic mix of jazz, chansons, world music and electronic tunes. Continuous music is played interrupted only for traffic announcements. There is no advertising and it is a cool vibe with smooth transitions between tunes.

Streams

128k MP3 stream

Location

Paris, France

Timezone

CET (UTC+1,UTC+2 daylight saving time)

Website

http://www.fipradio.fr

Programming Schedule

The broadcasts are presented live from 7 am to 11 pm, after which a computer replays a selection of the music broadcast earlier in the day
