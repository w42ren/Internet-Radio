WXYC

Originally on air in 1977 this is the campus radio station of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill WXYC radio. In 1994 it became the first radio station to stream its on air signal over the internet. WXYC has carried on its tradition of diverse and interesting programming playing blues jazz pop and dedicated programs for new music with new and interesting sounds from around the world.
